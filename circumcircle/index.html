Circumcircle

This is a really simple visual that shows the circumcircle of a triangle. It then colours the triangle depending on whether the mouse is located inside or outside the circumcircle.

The circumcircle is defined by a triangle with the three points of the triangle lying on the circumference. This also means that the bisectors of each of the triangles edges meet at the center of the circle.
